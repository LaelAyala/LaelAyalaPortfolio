Analyzing
Baseball Data

I used R to determine whether Left Handed Hitters have an advantage with being closer to first base. I looked at on base percentages, batting averages, and number of double plays to debunk the myth that Lefties hit better off of Right Handed Pitchers, but also reveal that being closer to first base does have a significant advantage in disrupting double plays.
